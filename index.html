Our Backstory

Conceived by a group of music aficionados with a penchant for puns, Melody Market began as a small record store nestled in the heart of the city. Fuelled by their shared love for all things melodic, the founders envisioned a space where music lovers could gather, explore, and celebrate the rich tapestry of musical expression. As the store grew from its humble beginnings, so did its reputation for offering an unparalleled selection of both vintage vinyl and the latest digital releases. Melody Market quickly became a cherished destination for audiophiles of all ages, drawn by its welcoming atmosphere and expertly curated collections.


Our Commitment to You

From vinyl to digital, Melody Market remains dedicated to honoring the diverse and ever-evolving landscape of music. With shelves stocked with rare finds and hidden gems, our passionate team delights in guiding customers on musical journeys of discovery. Whether it's recommending the perfect album for a cozy night in or sharing insights into emerging artists, we're here to nurture a love affair with music in all its forms. Our commitment extends beyond the store's walls, as we actively engage with the local music community through partnerships with artists, venues, and festivals. Through curated playlists, artist spotlights, and event collaborations, Melody Market strives to be a hub for musical inspiration and connection. With every visit to Melody Market, we invite you to immerse yourself in the boundless joy and endless possibilities of sound.
